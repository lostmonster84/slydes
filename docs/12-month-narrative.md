# 3. Slydes – 12-Month Narrative

## Purpose
This document maps the story an investor or acquirer should be able to tell after 12 months.

This is about inevitability, not vanity growth.

---

## Months 0–3: Format Validation

### Goal
Prove swipe-based web experiences outperform mobile pages.

### Focus
- Core editor polish
- Swipe depth tracking
- Completion rate tracking
- Performance optimisation

### Narrative
We proved users engage 3–5x deeper than traditional mobile pages.

---

## Months 3–6: Behavioural Lock-In

### Goal
Make Slydes the default mobile destination.

### Focus
- Intent-based templates
- QR and deep-link flows
- Bio and ad destinations
- Repeat usage

### Narrative
Slydes became the default endpoint for modern mobile traffic sources.

---

## Months 6–9: Conversion Proof

### Goal
Tie attention directly to outcomes.

### Focus
- Conversion overlays
- Intent-aware CTAs
- Lightweight A/B testing
- Performance comparisons

### Narrative
Slydes consistently outperformed mobile landing pages on conversion.

---

## Months 9–12: Category Ownership

### Goal
Own the category of mobile attention destinations.

### Focus
- Agency workflows
- White-label options
- APIs or lightweight SDKs
- Controlled expansion

### Narrative
Slydes defined a new mobile format and became the standard destination layer.

---

## What an Acquirer Sees

- Proven behavioural data
- Clear format ownership
- Strong mobile conversion metrics
- A product upstream of funnels

They do not see another builder.
They see leverage.

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Slydes operates as a **two-sided platform** connecting businesses (supply) with consumers (demand).

**Revenue Sources**:
1. **Subscription Revenue** (businesses pay monthly/annually)
2. **Transaction Fees** (commission on bookings facilitated)
3. **Featured Listings** (businesses pay for top placement in discovery)
4. **Enterprise & White Label** (custom solutions for large businesses)

**Economics**:
- High margin (70%+ gross margin targeting)
- SaaS predictability (recurring revenue)
- Network effects (more value = harder to leave)
- Viral growth (share = free acquisition)

### STREAM 3: FEATURED LISTINGS (Phase 3+)

**The Model**: Businesses pay to appear at top of discovery feed in Consumer App

---

#### How It Works

**Category-Based Featured Spots**:
- User opens Consumer App â†’ "Restaurants Near Me"
- Top 3 results are "Featured" (marked as sponsored)
- Businesses bid or pay fixed fee for placement

**Pricing Models**:

**Option A: Fixed Monthly**
- $299/month for featured placement in your category/location
- Limited spots (max 3 per category per city)

**Option B: CPC (Cost Per Click)**
- Pay $1-$3 per click on your Slyde in feed
- Set daily budget ($50/day = ~17-50 clicks)
- Performance-based (only pay for engagement)

**Option C: CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions)**
- Pay $10-$20 per 1,000 impressions in feed
- Good for brand awareness

**Free Tier**:

- **Watermark**: "Powered by Slydes" = organic marketing
- **Upgrade Trigger**: Remove watermark ($99/mo) = vanity upgrade

**Premium Tier ($199)**:
- **Signaling**: "I'm a premium brand" (ego play)

- **Margin**: Highest profit (minimal extra cost to deliver)

**Enterprise (Custom)**:
- **Psychology**: "If you have to ask, it's not for you"
- **Qualification**: Only serious, high-value customers inquire
- **Margin**: Highest (negotiated based on value)

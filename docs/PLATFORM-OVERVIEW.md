## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Slydes is a **two-sided discovery platform** for experience-first businesses.

**For Businesses**: Create immersive vertical video Slydes that capture attention like TikTok
**For Consumers**: Discover and book amazing experiences through an addictive swipe-to-explore app

**The Vision**: Become the default way people discover experiences ‚Äî restaurants, bars, hotels, venues, adventures, and everything worth experiencing. Replace fragmented discovery (Google, Yelp, Instagram) with one unified platform built entirely for mobile-first video storytelling.

## TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

### High-Level Stack

**Frontend**:
- **Web**: Next.js 14, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Mobile Apps**: React Native or Flutter (TBD)

**Backend**:
- **API**: Node.js / Next.js API routes
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (user data, business profiles, slides)
- **Media**: Cloudflare Stream (uploads, transcoding, HLS/DASH playback)
- **Images**: Cloudflare Images (uploads + automatic resizing/format)
- **Storage**: Cloudflare R2 (originals, posters, exports)
- **CDN/Edge**: Cloudflare (fast global delivery + security)

**Infrastructure**:
- **Hosting**: Vercel (web), AWS or Railway (backend)
- **Auth**: Supabase Auth or Clerk
- **Payments**: Stripe
- **Analytics**: Mixpanel or Amplitude

**Video Pipeline**:
- Upload ‚Üí Transcode (multiple resolutions) ‚Üí Stream playback (HLS/DASH) ‚Üí Edge delivery
- Optimization for mobile (vertical aspect ratio priority)
- Lazy loading & progressive streaming

## KEY RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Chicken & Egg Problem
**Risk**: Need businesses to attract consumers, need consumers to attract businesses
**Mitigation**: 
- Phase 1 focuses entirely on business value (shareable link, no app dependency)
- Only launch Consumer App after critical mass (1,000+ businesses)
- Seed initial consumer users with targeted local marketing

### Risk 2: Video Creation Barrier
**Risk**: Businesses may struggle to create quality video content
**Mitigation**:
- Simplified creation tools (templates, one-tap recording)
- Education content (how to film, lighting tips)
- Managed service tier (we create content for premium customers)

### Risk 3: Competitive Response
**Risk**: Instagram, TikTok, or others could copy the concept
**Mitigation**:
- First-mover advantage (lock in early customers)
- Vertical focus (we only serve businesses, not general social)
- Superior UX tailored to business needs
- Network effects create defensibility

### Risk 4: Monetization Timing
**Risk**: Asking businesses to pay too early could stunt growth
**Mitigation**:
- Freemium model in Phase 2 (free tier to attract volume)
- Influencer Program creates early distribution (not revenue)
- Clear value proposition (views, bookings, analytics)

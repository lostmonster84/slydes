## FEATURE PRIORITIZATION FRAMEWORK

### How We Decide What to Build

**Criteria**:
1. **Impact**: Does this 10x value for users?
2. **Effort**: Can we ship it in <1 month?
3. **Alignment**: Does it move us toward network effect?
4. **Differentiation**: Does this make us unique?

**Prioritization Matrix**:
- **High Impact + Low Effort** = Ship NOW
- **High Impact + High Effort** = Plan for next quarter
- **Low Impact + Low Effort** = Nice-to-have (backlog)
- **Low Impact + High Effort** = Don't build

## RISKS & CONTINGENCIES

### Risk 1: Slower Customer Acquisition
**Mitigation**: 
- Focus on single vertical (restaurants only) to prove concept
- Increase marketing spend
- Offer incentives (first month free, discounts)

### Risk 2: Partner App Adoption Low
**Mitigation**:
- Maintain manual onboarding option (hybrid model)
- Simplify app UX based on feedback
- Offer training/support

### Risk 3: Consumer App Doesn't Gain Traction
**Mitigation**:
- Delay launch until 2,000+ businesses (more content)
- Seed with targeted local campaigns (NYC, LA, Miami)
- Influencer partnerships for initial users

### Risk 4: Funding Shortfall
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on profitability earlier (reduce burn)
- Bootstrap longer (delay hires)
- Bridge round from angels

## üì± Mobile-First Implementation

### Design Principles

**1. Vertical Everything**
- Full viewport height sections
- No horizontal scrolling
- Thumb-friendly tap targets
- Swipe gestures enabled

**2. Video-First**
- Every section has motion
- Auto-play (muted) videos
- Smooth transitions
- Ken Burns effects on stills

**3. Minimal Text**
- Headlines: 10 words max
- Body: 20 words max
- Let visuals do the talking
- CTA clarity over explanation

**4. Fast as Hell**
- < 1 second load time
- Lazy load everything below fold
- Optimized video delivery
- No janky animations

## üîÑ Interaction Design

### Micro-interactions That Matter

**Scroll Behavior**:
- Snap to sections (full-screen frames)
- Parallax backgrounds (subtle)
- Fade in/out animations
- Progress indicator (side dots)

**Button Hovers**:
- Scale up (1.02)
- Gradient shift
- Shadow enhance
- Smooth 200ms transition

**Video Triggers**:
- Auto-play when 50% in viewport
- Mute/unmute toggle
- Loop seamlessly
- Pause when scrolled past

**Form Interactions**:
- Inline validation (instant)
- Success animations
- Error shake (subtle)
- Focus states (Leader Blue ring)
